# Retail-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard

📊 Dashboard Overview

The Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution developed in Microsoft Excel to monitor and analyze retail sales performance. It consolidates raw sales data into meaningful insights using PivotTables, PivotCharts, KPI Cards, and Slicers, enabling users to explore sales performance across different dimensions.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of key business metrics, including Sales Revenue, Net Profit, Total Cost, Total Orders, and Average Delivery Time. Interactive slicers allow users to filter data by Year, Month, Country, and Product, making it easy to perform detailed analysis and compare business performance across different segments.

📌 Dashboard Components

📈 KPI Cards

The KPI cards provide an at-a-glance summary of the business by displaying:
Sales Revenue
Net Profit
Total Cost
Total Orders
Average Delivery Time

📅 Monthly Sales Trend
A line chart visualizes monthly Sales Revenue, Total Cost, and Net Profit, helping identify seasonal patterns, revenue fluctuations, and profitability trends over time.

📦 Product Category Performance
A clustered column chart compares Sales Revenue, Total Cost, and Net Profit across different product categories, helping identify the most profitable categories.

🏆 Top Products Analysis
A horizontal bar chart highlights the Top 10 Products based on sales revenue, enabling quick identification of best-selling products.

💳 Payment Method Analysis
A bar chart compares sales generated through different payment methods, providing insights into customer payment preferences.

🚚 Order Status Distribution
A pie chart displays the distribution of Delivered, Shipped, and Delayed orders, helping evaluate order fulfillment performance.

🎛 Interactive Filters

Slicers enable dynamic filtering by:
Year
Month
Country
Product
This allows users to instantly update all charts and KPIs for focused business analysis.

💼 Business Value

This dashboard enables businesses to:
Monitor overall sales and profitability.
Track monthly sales performance and trends.
Identify high-performing product categories and products.
Analyze customer payment preferences.
Evaluate order fulfillment performance.
Support data-driven decision-making through interactive reporting.
